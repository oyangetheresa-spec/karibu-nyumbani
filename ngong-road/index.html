How to stop "Runaways" and Damage: 1. **The "Security Deposit" (Refundable):** Instead of just the nightly rate, charge a refundable damage deposit (e.g., 2,000 Ksh). Tell them: *"This is returned within 2 hours of checkout after a room inspection."* People who intend to break things will refuse to pay this. 2. **Smart Locks:** If possible, invest in a simple digital keypad lock. You only give the code *after* the ID is uploaded and the deposit is paid. You can also "expire" the code at 11:00 AM on checkout day. 3. **Night-Time Security:** Explicitly state on the page: *"24/7 Gate Security with CCTV."* Even if it’s just standard building security, mentioning it deters criminals. #### B. Dealing with ID Refusal: Frame it as **Government Compliance** rather than your own rule. * *Change your text to:* "Per Kenyan Law and Building Security requirements, all guests must submit a Government ID prior to check-in. Handling No-Shows: 1. **Partial Payment to Confirm:** Never hold a room for free. 2. *Rule:* "Your booking is only confirmed once a 50% reservation deposit is received.
