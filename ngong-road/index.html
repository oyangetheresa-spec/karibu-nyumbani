Booking Requirements & Security

To ensure a safe and high-quality environment for all our guests, please note our standard check-in process:

1. Verified Identification

Per building security regulations, a valid Government-issued ID must be submitted via our secure digital form prior to arrival.

2. Reservation Deposit

A 50% reservation deposit is required to secure your dates. This ensures your apartment is blocked exclusively for you.

3. Refundable Security Fee

A small refundable damage deposit (Ksh 2,000) is required. This is processed back to you within 2 hours of a successful room inspection at checkout.
